About Us

OS is proud of its music heritage. Director Jamie Wood cut his teeth designing back stage areas and after parties for the Rolling Stones - and this spirit of style and vision is still evident in Outer Sanctum today.

It's a unique perspective - creative to the core, and able to call on a powerful network of contacts - we make memorable things happen for our clients.

Talent excites us, and we have the very best designers, technicians and engineers in the business. Working with OS means working with a highly experienced team of creatives and strategists. We get a kick out of pushing the boundaries of what's possible to create memorable events and ingenious brand partnerships.
